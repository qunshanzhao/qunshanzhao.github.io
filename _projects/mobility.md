We analyse how people and vehicles move through cities: high-resolution traffic flow data from Glasgow's urban traffic control system, electric vehicle charging behaviour, e-scooters and shared mobility. Agent-based and reinforcement-learning models let us test transport policy scenarios before they reach the street.

See the [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page for related papers, including the Glasgow traffic flow dataset in *Scientific Data* (2025).
