Using energy performance certificates (EPC), administrative records and emerging urban big data, we study the energy efficiency of the housing stock, fuel poverty, and how much households are willing to pay for energy-efficient homes — with deep learning models applied at city scale in Glasgow and evidence drawn from the 2022 energy crisis in the UK.

See the [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page for related papers in *Energy and Buildings* (2022) and *Energy Research & Social Science* (2026).
