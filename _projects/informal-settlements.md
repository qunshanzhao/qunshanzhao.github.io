A large share of urban growth happens in informal settlements that rarely appear on official maps. Working with the [IDEAMaps](https://www.ideamapsnetwork.org/) network, we combine Earth observation, machine learning and community knowledge to map informal settlements and deprived urban areas in African cities including Nairobi, Lagos and Kano — producing open data and methods that support local decision-making.

See the [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page for related papers.
