Where should a city place street trees, bike-sharing stations, refuelling infrastructure or public services so they do the most good? We develop location–allocation and other spatial optimisation methods for urban infrastructure — from tree shade coverage to service siting — and ship them as open-source software in [PySAL/spopt](https://github.com/pysal/spopt), which I help maintain.

See the [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page for related papers, including the [spopt paper in JOSS](https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.03330) (2022), and the [open source]({{ '/opensource/' | relative_url }}) page for the software itself.
