What can foundation models and GeoAI do for urban research? We explore zero-shot prediction of urban indicators, captioning and interpretation of remote-sensing imagery, and large-scale 3D city modelling — including open 3D building model datasets constructed from airborne LiDAR point clouds for Glasgow.

See the [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page for related papers, including the Glasgow 3D building datasets in *Environment and Planning B* (2026).
