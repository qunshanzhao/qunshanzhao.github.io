Cities concentrate both opportunity and disadvantage. With third-sector and public partners we analyse programmes that support people facing hardship — such as the Salvation Army's Employment Plus programme and utility assistance for heat resilience — and study health equity in access to services. A second strand explores the social and linguistic life of cities: combining GPS, recorders, and map-based ethnographic interviews to understand multilingual communities, and supporting Glasgow's Gaelic speakers through the Gaelic Connect digital hub.
