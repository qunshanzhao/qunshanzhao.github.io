Since 2019, the University of Glasgow has invested substantial dedicated funding to develop Data Science and AI across the whole university. Beyond hiring in the traditional data science and AI disciplines — computer science and statistics — the university has also invested heavily in urban/social science and geographic information science, to drive interdisciplinary integration between these fields, data science, and AI.

Across Geography and Urban Studies, Glasgow now has a combined urban/geographic-information data science and GIS/RS community of roughly 15–20 people, with more related positions (faculty, postdoc, and PhD) expected over the next 1–2 years.

## Urban Studies & the Urban Big Data Centre

**Phase one (2014–19):**

- **Prof. Nick Bailey** (University of Cambridge, UK) — housing and neighbourhoods, administrative big data
- **Dr Jinhyun Hong**, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Urban Planning, University of Washington, USA) — *2022 update: Jin has moved back to the University of Seoul in South Korea but remains a UBDC affiliate.*
- **Dr Jing Yao**, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Geography, Arizona State University, USA) — GIScience, spatial statistics, spatial optimization, location modeling, health geography, regional science, urban and regional planning
- **Dr David McArthur**, Reader (PhD: Business Economics, Norwegian School of Economics, Norway) — transportation, econometrics

**Phase two (2019–2024):**

- **Dr Qunshan Zhao**, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Geography, Arizona State University, USA) — GIScience, remote sensing, spatial analysis, location modeling and analysis, urban analytics, urban data science
- **Prof. Joao Porto de Albuquerque** (PhD: Computer Science, Unicamp, Brazil & TU Dortmund, Germany) — urban analytics, sustainable development, GIS, information systems, urban/disaster resilience
- **Prof. Alison Heppenstall** (PhD: GIS and AI, University of Leeds, UK) — geocomputation, machine learning, geospatial AI, agent-based modeling, smart cities
- **Dr Mark Livingston**, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Public Health, University of Glasgow) — housing analytics, private rented sector — *2024 update: Mark retired in April 2024.*
- **Dr Fiona Crawford**, Lecturer (PhD: Transportation, University of Leeds) — travel behaviour, transportation planning
- **Dr Michael Sinclair**, Lecturer (PhD: Natural Resources & Environmental Management, University of Haifa) — human–nature interaction, new forms of data
- **Dr Jose Rafael Verduzco-Torres**, Lecturer (PhD: Urban Studies, University of Glasgow) — public transport accessibility, land economy and policy
