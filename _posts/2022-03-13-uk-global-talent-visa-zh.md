最近因为要帮中心招聘一位新的博士后,就涉及到帮博士后办理英国工作签证的问题。和学校 HR 交流了一下信息,发现这个从原来的 tier1 修改更新之后的签证其实对于英国学术界招聘人才是一个很大的利好。具体的签证信息可以见英国政府的官网:Apply for the Global Talent visa (不得不说英国政府的各种签证办理的网页还是做得非常细致周到的)

这个签证主要分两步,第一步是拿到 endorsement,大概就是证明你是个人才,第二步是具体的签证申请流程。

这个 endorsement 有四种方法,一是拿到副教授以上教职或者机构高级研究岗位,二是拿到一些比较难申请的个人科研奖学金,三是拿到通过由英国政府或者别的得到认证的基金会资助的博士后工作,四是通过同行评审。在这四个选项里,第三个是相对最为容易的。只要是申请人有博士学位,项目总时长超过 2 年而且项目总额超过 3w 英镑,就可以直接申请这个endorsement。

如果能通过前三项 endorsement 拿到签证,就可以三年办理英国永居。通过第四项拿到 endorsement 的有可能需要 3年或者 5年才能拿到永居。总体来说对于希望能稳定在英国做学术研究的人才,尤其是非热门专业来说还是十分利好的。这项政策某种程度上是因为在脱欧之后英国希望吸引更多高素质人才,来替代以前从欧陆自由流动过来的人才,相似的政策也包括恢复英国高校学生毕业之后可以留英工作 2 年的 PSW 签证。

全球英才签证比起普通的 tier2 工签还是有很多好处的,比如说 tier2 工签只能办理 3 年或者 5 年,global talent visa 最短可以办理一年,最长可以办理五年。Tier2 工签需要提供英语语言证明,敏感专业还需要通过 ATAS 审核,global talent visa没有这些要求,所以签证的申请速度会相对更快一些。而且 Global Talent Visa 也可以自雇或者干别的工作,只要是和专业相关就可以。

上面我只是简单的用中文解释了一下,下面是一些具体的英文解释,供大家参考。因为 global talent visa 是可以把一些别的类型的签证居留时间算在办理永居的时间里的,所以如果已经在 tier2 签证快要呆满三年而且满足 global talent visa 的条件,可以直接申请换一个一年的 global talent visa签证然后直接申请永居,就不用到呆满五年再申请了。目前来看这个签证并不难申请,不知道以后英国政府会不会收紧政策,比如说把各方面的要求逐渐提高,来控制申请人数。

Academic and research appointments (fast-track) - endorsement for individuals who have accepted a job in which they will have responsibility for academic, research or innovation leadership, or are an emerging leader. This includes but is not limited to Professor, Associate Professor and Senior Group Leaders appointments. The appointment must be at an approved UK higher education institution or research institute. Certain recruitment requirements must also be met. This route is administered by the British Academy, Royal Academy of Engineering and Royal Society.

Individual fellowships (fast-track) – endorsement for individuals who have been awarded an individual fellowship on the list approved by the British Academy, Royal Academy of Engineering and Royal Society. The fellowship must be held currently or within the last 12 months.

Endorsed funders (fast-track) – endorsement for researchers and specialists, employed or hosted by a department, faculty or college, whose name or job title is specified in a successful grant application from an endorsed funder approved by UKRI.

Peer-reviewed application (standard) – endorsement application for individuals who meet all eligibility criteria and demonstrate they are proven leader within their field of expertise (Exceptional Talent) or have the potential to become a leader within their field of expertise (Exceptional Promise). The application is assessed by fellows at the British Academy, Royal Academy of Engineering or Royal Society.

The most utilised route is the Endorsed Funder. The eligibility of this route is detailed below:

· You have a grant award that is provided by an Endorsed Funder as shown in the Immigration rules here

· Your grant award is worth £30,000 (Note: this is the total amount, not a per annum or salary equivalent)

· Your grant award is for a minimum duration of 24 months, including the time left on the contract

· Your grant award names you as the applicant under directly incurred costs
