从2019年以来,英国格拉斯哥大学投入了大量专项资金,重点投资与发展全校层面的数据科学(Data Science)和人工智能(AI)方向。除了在计算机系和统计系等传统数据科学与AI学科的招聘之外,学校在城市/社会科学以及地理信息科学方向同时投入了大量资金,以推动这些学科与数据科学和AI的跨学科融合和发展。

目前在格大地理系与城市研究系,形成了总共15-20人左右的城市/地理信息数据科学以及GIS/RS团队,未来1-2年还会有更多的相关职位(包括教职,博士后,和博士生岗位)。

城市研究系与城市大数据中心:

第一阶段(2014-19):

Prof. Nick Bailey (University of Cambridge, UK)
Nick Bailey: housing and neighborhood, administrative big data

Dr Jinhyun Hong, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Urban Planning, University of Washington, USA)
2022 update: Jin has moved back to the University of Seoul in South Korea but continues to be an affiliate of UBDC.

Dr Jing Yao, Senior Lecturer (PhD: Geography, Arizona State University, USA)
Jing Yao: Geographical Information Science, Spatial Statistics, Spatial Optimization, Location Modeling, Health Geography, Regional Science, Urban and Regional Planning and development

Dr David McArthur, Reader (PhD: Business Economics, Norwegian School of Economics, Norway)
David McArthur: transportation, econometrics
